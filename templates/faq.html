FAQ

Are the cars insured?

Yes, all our cars have Third-Party Insurance. Also, you can add extra insurance for an extra charge.


Can I change the car after the reservation?

Yes, you can. If you want to change or cancel your reservation please call (+91) 90225522 or email us at phantomservice@gmail.com


Do I need an international driving permit to rent a car?

It depends on what country you’re from. If you’re not an EU citizen, you probably need an international driving permit. *Americans, Canadians, and Australians must have an international driving permit to rent any motor vehicle in Santorini.
If you’re an EU citizen you are not required to have an international driving permit.


Where can I pick up the car rental and where should I return it?

When you choose the car, please select pick up and drop off locations. For example, the options are any hotel, Santorini Airport, Athinios Port, or Fira Cable Car.
